# Introduction to Public Health Informatics Course


## Course Details

- **Course Name:** Introduction to Public Health Informatics


## Course Description

Welcome to the world of **Introduction to Public Health Informatics**! This course is designed to provide you with a foundational understanding of the role of informatics in public health, as well as key concepts in public health, health promotion, disease prevention, ethics, and more.

## Topics Covered

- Introduction to Public Health
  - Health Promotion and Disease Prevention
  - Role of Government in Health
  - Public Health Policies and Interventions

- Global Health and Health Inequities
  - Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness
  - Environmental Issues in Public Health

- Mapping and Surveillance Techniques for Disease Control

- Introduction to Biomedical Informatics
  - Role of Informatics in Public Health and Global Health
  - Standards in Biomedical and Public Health Informatics
  - Evaluation of Biomedical and Health Information Resources

- Patient-Centered Care Systems and Patient Monitoring Systems

- Clinical Research Informatics
  - Introduction to the Clinical Research Process
  - WHO Principles of Good Clinical Practice
  - Ethical Conduct and Protocol Compliance

- Introduction to Ethics in Healthcare
  - Issues in Clinical Ethics and Research Ethics
  - Ethical Issues in Healthcare Technology
